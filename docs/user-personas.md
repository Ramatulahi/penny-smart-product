# User Personas

## Persona 1: The Student

Age: 20–25  
Occupation: University Student

Goals:
- Track daily expenses
- Manage limited income
- Save small amounts regularly

Pain Points:
- Difficulty tracking spending
- Lack of budgeting discipline

---

## Persona 2: The Young Professional

Age: 23–30  
Occupation: Entry-Level Professional

Goals:
- Track salary and monthly expenses
- Build savings habits
- Monitor financial health

Pain Points:
- Overspending
- Lack of financial visibility
